Business-Specific Financial Statements

Automated financial statements generation as per business type and statutory requirements

As per Companies Act 2013

Private Limited Company

Required Statements

  • Balance Sheet (Schedule III Format)
  • Statement of Profit and Loss
  • Cash Flow Statement (AS-3)
  • Notes to Accounts (1-25)

Compliance Requirements

  • CARO 2020 Applicability
  • Directors' Report
  • Consolidated Statements (if applicable)
  • Board's Report

Applicable Standards

  • AS-1 to AS-29 (If IGAAP)
  • IND-AS (If applicable)
  • Schedule III Disclosures
As per Partnership Act

Partnership Firm

Required Statements

  • Trading Account
  • Profit and Loss Account
  • Balance Sheet
  • Partners' Capital Accounts

Special Considerations

  • Partner Salary & Commission
  • Interest on Capital
  • Profit Sharing Ratio
  • Current Account Adjustments

Applicable Standards

  • Basic Accounting Standards
  • Partnership Deed Terms
  • State-specific Requirements
As per Income Tax Act

Individual/Proprietorship

Required Statements

  • Statement of Affairs
  • Income and Expenditure Account
  • Capital Account
  • Drawing Account

Tax Compliance

  • ITR-3/4 Requirements
  • Presumptive Taxation (44AD/44ADA)
  • Tax Audit (if applicable)

Documentation

  • Basic Accounting Records
  • Bank Reconciliation
  • Asset Register
As per LLP Act 2008

Limited Liability Partnership

Required Statements

  • Statement of Assets & Liabilities
  • Statement of Income & Expenditure
  • Partners' Account
  • Annual Return

Statutory Requirements

  • Statement of Account & Solvency
  • Annual Return (Form 11)
  • Income Tax Return

Applicable Guidelines

  • LLP Agreement Terms
  • Accounting Standards
  • MCA Guidelines
